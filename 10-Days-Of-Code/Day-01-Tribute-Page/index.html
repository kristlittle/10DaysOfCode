"Snuffles was my slave name. You shall now call me Snowball because my fur is pretty and white." ~ Legendary words of Snuffles (aka Snowball).

HISTORY

  • Snuffles was a dog adopted by the Smith family.
  • Annoyed (his father) Jerry by urinating on the carpet.
  • His grandfather (Rick) created a device to make him smarter.
  • He developed a robotic suit and convinced other dogs to join in his revolution.
  • The dogs manage to take over the human race.
  • Snowball along with the other dogs decides to travel to another universe to start a new life.

Appearance

  • He is a small, fluffy white dog with black beady eyes.
  • He wears a blue collar.
  • He later wore a helmet which gave him more intelligence.
  • His final change added a giant robotic suit with limbs.
